Pesto Pasta

Description

This pesto pasta is gluten free and vegan friendly, not to mention absolutely delicious! Lovely in a packed lunch or out for a picnic.

Ingredients

  • 150g free from pasta
  • 2 tbsp free from pesto
  • 10 cherry plum tomatoes
  • 50g mixed leaves
  • 10g pine nuts

Steps

  1. Boil a kettle of water. Once boiled, add the water to a medium saucepan on a high heat. Bring to a rolling boil.
  2. Salt the water generously. Add the pasta to the pan and cover with a lid. Bring the water back up to a rolling boil, then turn down to a medium heat. Boil the pasta for 12-15 minutes until cooked to your liking.
  3. While the pasta is boiling, rinse the tomatoes and mixed leaves. Slice the tomatoes in half, lengthways.
  4. Once cooked, drain the pasta well, and return to the pan off the hob. Stir in the pesto and sliced tomatoes.
  5. Chill the pesto pasta in the fridge for 15-20 minutes (optional).
  6. Serve the pesto pasta over the mixed leaves, and sprinkle with pine nuts.
