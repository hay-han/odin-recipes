Nutty Berry Porridge

Description

This delectable porridge is filled with sweet berries and delicious, creamy peanut butter to set you up for the day!

The peanut butter packs a punch with healthy fats and protein; the berries provide an excellent helping of antioxidants.

Only question is, are you a crunchy or smooth peanut butter person?

Ingredients

  • 50g organic porridge oats
  • 150ml soy milk alternative
  • 1 medjool date
  • 1 tsp ground flaxseed
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 10 raspberries
  • 10 blueberries
  • 10g nuts/coconut shavings/seeds to serve
